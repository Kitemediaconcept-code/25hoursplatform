Designed for the 25th Hour

We believe that everyone has an extra hour hidden in their daily routine. By eliminating fluff, pre-recorded drone courses, and academic gatekeeping, we help you capture that hour and convert it into career-defining skills.

Our methodology focuses on extreme utility: short, punchy practitioner-led cohorts, robust git-based capstone portfolios, and active peer reviews. When you graduate, you don't just get a PDF credential—you get a validated repository that proves you can build real products.
