About this class

Many startup guides offer clean generalities. This course offers tactical metrics. Led by active founder and early-stage venture investor Rahul Menon, this professional path equips you with actual frameworks to test assumptions, structure financial sheets, and build initial models.

By the end of this course, you will have structured a validated venture concept, designed a cold-email B2B acquisition pipeline, and drafted a cohesive investor pitch deck ready to present to early funders.
